HostProof

Privacy Policy

Effective: June 28, 2026

HostProof is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information. When you register, we collect your email address and display name. This information is stored securely to manage your account and provide our services.

1.2 Property and Inspection Data. To deliver the App's core features, we collect and store:

  • Property details you enter (address, property name, description)
  • Tenant information you add (names, check-in and check-out dates)
  • Inspection records (dates, notes, condition ratings)
  • Photos and images you capture or upload within the App

1.3 Location Data. With your permission, the App captures GPS coordinates when you take inspection photos. This location data is embedded in photo metadata as geographic evidence. We do not track your location in the background.

1.4 Device and Technical Information. We collect basic technical information such as device type, operating system version, and App version to maintain compatibility and diagnose issues. This does not personally identify you.

1.5 Usage and Error Data. We may collect anonymised crash reports and error logs to improve App stability. This data does not include your personal inspection content.

1.6 Payment Information. We do not collect or store your payment card details. Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status (active or inactive) via RevenueCat.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App and its features
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Generate inspection reports and PDF documents on your behalf
  • Store your inspection records and make them accessible across sessions
  • Send inspection reminders (only if you enable notifications)
  • Respond to your support requests and enquiries
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or unauthorised activity
  • Comply with applicable laws and legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

HostProof uses the following third-party services to operate. Each has its own privacy policy:

Supabase — Database, Authentication & Storage

Your account data, property records, inspection data, and photos are stored on Supabase-managed infrastructure with industry-standard encryption.

supabase.com/privacy

RevenueCat — Subscription Management

RevenueCat manages and validates your Pro subscription status. It receives your App Store purchase receipt and returns your subscription state to the App. No payment card data is shared.

revenuecat.com/privacy

Apple App Store — Payments

All subscription payments are handled directly by Apple. We do not have access to your payment card information.

apple.com/legal/privacy

Sentry — Error Monitoring (Development Only)

Sentry is used in development builds to track application errors. It is disabled in production App Store builds. No user content is transmitted to Sentry.

sentry.io/privacy

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure, encrypted servers managed by Supabase. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

All data transmissions between the App and our servers are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Photos are stored in private, access-controlled cloud storage accessible only by your account.

While we take data security seriously, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. If you delete your account:

  • Your account credentials are permanently removed
  • Your property and inspection records are deleted from our active database
  • Your uploaded photos are removed from storage

Some data may be retained in encrypted backups for up to 90 days before being permanently purged. To request immediate deletion of all your data, contact us at hostproof.support.in@gmail.com.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access. You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Correction. You may update your name and email directly in the App (Settings → Edit Name / Change Password).

Deletion. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting hostproof.support.in@gmail.com.

Data Portability. Upon request, we will provide your inspection data in a portable format where technically feasible.

Objection. You may object to certain processing of your data by contacting us.

To exercise any of these rights, email hostproof.support.in@gmail.com with the subject line "Privacy Request." We aim to respond within 7 business days.

7. Location Permissions

HostProof requests access to your device's location only when you take inspection photos (to embed GPS coordinates as evidence). We use "While Using the App" location access only — we never access your location in the background.

You can revoke location permission at any time in your device Settings. Revoking access will not affect existing photos but new photos will not contain GPS metadata.

8. Camera and Photo Library

The App requests access to your device camera and photo library solely to capture and import inspection photos. Photos you take or import are uploaded to your private account storage. We do not access your photo library for any other purpose.

9. Push Notifications

With your permission, HostProof sends local push notifications to remind you of upcoming property check-ins or checkouts. These notifications are generated locally on your device and do not involve transmitting personal data to our servers. You can disable notifications at any time in your device Settings or within the App (Settings → Notifications).

10. Children's Privacy

HostProof is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us at hostproof.support.in@gmail.com and we will promptly delete it.

11. International Data Transfers

Our service providers (Supabase, RevenueCat) may process and store data in jurisdictions outside your country of residence, including the United States and the European Union. These providers implement appropriate safeguards to protect data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of material changes via an in-app notification or by email. Your continued use of HostProof after the effective date of an updated policy constitutes your acceptance of the changes.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
